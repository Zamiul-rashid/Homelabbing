## Multi-Container Architecture

Immich relies on a robust microservice stack that our `stacks/photo-backup/docker-compose.yml` configures automatically:

- **`immich-server` (`:2283`)** — Core API, web frontend, and upload controller.
- **`immich-machine-learning`** — Python service running CLIP and face detection models locally.
- **`immich-redis`** — High-speed task queue and job caching.
- **`immich-db` (`pgvecto-rs`)** — PostgreSQL database with vector search capabilities for instant semantic photo lookups.

## Step-by-Step Setup & Mobile Backup

### 1. Launching the Stack
Navigate to `stacks/photo-backup/` and start the containers:
```bash
cd stacks/photo-backup
docker compose up -d
```

### 2. First-Time Registration
Navigate to `http://YOUR_SERVER_IP:2283` and click **Get Started**. Enter your email, full name, and password to create the initial admin account.

### 3. Connecting the Mobile App
1. Download **Immich** from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
2. Open the app and enter your server address: `http://YOUR_SERVER_IP:2283` (or your remote HTTPS URL).
3. Log in and toggle on **Foreground & Background Backup**. Watch as your phone backs up thousands of photos at lightning speed over your local Wi-Fi!
