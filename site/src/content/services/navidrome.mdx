## What You'll Have When Done
A blazing-fast, private music server that indexes your entire FLAC, MP3, and ALAC audio collection. You can stream lossless audio directly from any web browser or connect dozens of sleek third-party mobile apps (`Symfonium`, `Substreamer`, `Amperfy`) for offline downloading, smart playlists, and scrobbling without monthly subscription fees.

## Why Choose Navidrome?

Audio streaming platforms compress your music, remove classic albums due to licensing disputes, and charge recurring monthly fees. Navidrome runs on less than 50 MB of RAM while serving bit-perfect audio directly from your hard drive.

## Onboarding & Mobile App Connection

### 1. Launching the Container
Once you place your audio files in `/data/media/music`, run `docker compose up -d` in `stacks/music-server/`:
```bash
cd stacks/music-server
docker compose up -d
```

### 2. Initial Scan & Login
Open `http://YOUR_SERVER_IP:4533`. Create your admin profile (`username` and `password`). Navidrome immediately begins scanning `/data/media/music` and populates your album art in seconds.

### 3. Recommended Mobile Clients
Because Navidrome implements the industry-standard **Subsonic API**, you aren't forced into using a single proprietary app:

- **Android:** We highly recommend **Symfonium** (unmatched equalizer, offline caching, and widget support) or **Substreamer** (free and minimalist).
- **iOS:** We recommend **Amperfy** (free, designed to feel exactly like Apple Music) or **Substreamer**.

To connect: open the app, enter `http://YOUR_SERVER_IP:4533` (or your domain/Tailscale URL), and sign in with your Navidrome credentials!
