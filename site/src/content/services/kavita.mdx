## Onboarding & Library Setup

### 1. Launching the Container
Once your books and comics are placed in `/data/media/books` and `/data/media/comics`, run `docker compose up -d` in `stacks/book-reader/`:
```bash
cd stacks/book-reader
docker compose up -d
```

### 2. Creating Your Admin Account
Open `http://YOUR_SERVER_IP:5000` in your web browser. You will be greeted by the initial setup screen where you create your admin profile username and password.

### 3. Adding Your Libraries
1. In the Kavita dashboard, click **Server Settings (Gear icon)** → **Libraries** → **+ Add Library**.
2. **Name:** enter `Books` or `Comics`.
3. **Type:** choose `Book` for novels/EPUBs, or `Comic/Manga` for CBZ/CBR archives.
4. **Folders:** browse to `/data/media/books` (or `/data/media/comics`), select the folder, and click **Save**.
5. Kavita will perform a lightning-fast background scan and build your digital bookshelf!
